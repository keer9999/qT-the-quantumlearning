The Variational Method

What is the Variational Method?

The variational method is an approximation technique used to estimate the ground-state energy of a quantum system when exact solutions are not available.

A trial wavefunction with adjustable parameters is chosen. By minimizing the expectation value of the Hamiltonian, the best approximation to the ground state is obtained.
