Correct, congratulations! A superposition state will have contributions of the ground state as well as excited states. Thus, the average energy ... will be greater than E0. Mathematically, for an energy superposition state ... = c0 E0 + c1 E1 + c2E2 + ..., with at least two non-zero c_i and where the modulus squared of these coefficients adds to 1 due to normalization. This sum is always greater than E0.
