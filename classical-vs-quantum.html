Classical Bit vs Quantum Bit (Qubit)

Understanding information: Classical vs Quantum

What is a Classical Bit?

A classical bit is the smallest unit of information in classical computers. It can be in only one state at a time:

0
1

Examples:

  • Light switch: ON or OFF
  • Door: Open or Closed
  • Electric signal: Low or High voltage

What is a Qubit?
